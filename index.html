About ME

I am a student and an aspiring developer who is passionate and persistent in developing himself in the field of IT and technology. Since childhood I was attracted by devices that can be not only disassembled, but also improved and then create something of my own. Today, my hobbies are programming, electronics, and 3D modelling. They have become the basis of my professional growth and creative fulfilment. I study new trends and technologies, strive to delve into theory and practice. I like to bring projects to completion and see how ideas become working products - be it an electronic device, a website or a 3D model. Many of the skills I have learnt on my own, by doing practical tasks, participating in competitions and my own project. I believe that learning is a lifelong process, and I am always trying to improve my skills.
